About HOME CUCINE

HOME CUCINE produces highly versatile classic, modern and luxury modular kitchens. The company produces laminate, wooden, lacquered kitchens and caters to trade intermediaries and furniture dealers in general. Home Cucine stands out for its excellent quality/price ratio and its kitchens’ certified safety. Quality in safety. Home Cucine’s production site is in Cison di Valmarino (Treviso), a picturesque village at the foot of a medieval castle where all our products are designed and then translated into reality, Made in Italy. Welcome to Home Cucine.

To produce kitchen furniture for domestic use that satisfy the expectations of our customers in terms of safety, functionality, quality/price ratio. Through continuous improvement of internal expertise, Home intends to offer customers services also highly innovative, without losing sight of the interests of all stakeholders of the company (particularly the employees) and of the environmental protection.
